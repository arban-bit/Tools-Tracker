How to set up your site's toolkit:
  1. Add or select your wind farm in the Wind Farms section below
  2. Select the V236 Standard template
  3. Export as CSV — opens in Excel
  4. Edit the file: add, remove, or rename tools to match your site
  5. Create a new template (+ New Template), then import your CSV
  6. Assign your wind farm to the new template
